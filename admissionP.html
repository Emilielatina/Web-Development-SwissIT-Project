Welcome!

Thank you for your interest in Crimson High.

Crimson High is a school that promotes excellence and world ready learners of today. We know that learning is a part of growing yet we also took consideration of growing learners who is ready to step forward to a global competitive world. This is why Crimson High provides quality education for its student true to its mission and expectation. Our school makes sure that we promote camaraderie, respect and readiness because we believe that in order for a person to succeed, he needs to cooperate and help, which creates harmony of excellence. Thus, respect is given for everyone who knows how to help and give. Readiness, is what we want for our learners. Readiness to help, work hard and excel in their own way. Crimson High is a school that we need today and we believe that in Crimson High we can fly High.



Emilie Latina
Crimson High President
